About me

Hello my name is daniel and I was born on 06.02.1994 in Bregenz. After my compulsory schooling I did an apprenticeship as an electrical systems engineer at Doppelmayr Seilbahnen. After I finished this apprenticeship I worked for 3 months at my father's company. After the 3 months I continued my work as an electrical installation technician at Greussing Kranservice. There my activities included hardware planning, software development and project management of crane renovations on drilling platforms and ships. There I worked with various visualization programs, tools for robotic, tools for various drive systems and with Beckhoff controllers. Through these activities I developed my passion for software development and since then I work at home on small software and server projects. That's why I decided to study at the Coding-Campus.
